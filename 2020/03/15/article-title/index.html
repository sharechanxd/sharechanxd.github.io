测试1

$$
\phi(z)=1-1.2z+0.85 z^{2}
$$
And we can get $\phi_{1}=1.2,\phi_{2}=-0.85,z_{1}=0.7058824+0.8235294i,z_{2}= 0.7058824-0.8235294i$,$z_{1}=\bar{z}{2}$ and we consider the initial conditions that $\rho(0)=1$ and $\rho(-1)=\frac{\phi{1}}{1-\phi_{2}}=\frac{24}{37}$.And here we take $\rho(h)=c_{1} z_{1}^{-h}+\bar{c_{2}} z_{2}^{-h}$. Plug these in and we can get:$\rho(h)=(0.5+0.034749i)(0.7058824+0.8235294i)^{-h}+(0.5-0.034749i)(0.7058824-0.8235294i)^{-h}$. And we plot the acf:

$$
\begin{eqnarray}
\nabla\cdot\vec{E} &=& \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0} \
\nabla\cdot\vec{B} &=& 0 \
\nabla\times\vec{E} &=& -\frac{\partial B}{\partial t} \
\nabla\times\vec{B} &=& \mu_0\left(\vec{J}+\epsilon_0\frac{\partial E}{\partial t} \right)
\end{eqnarray}
$$
